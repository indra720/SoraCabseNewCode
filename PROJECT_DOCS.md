# Sora Cabs Enterprise ERP - Documentation

Welcome to the Sora Cabs Enterprise ERP documentation. This platform is a comprehensive, multi-tenant SaaS solution designed to manage every aspect of modern mobility, restaurant, and fleet operations.

---

### 1. Dashboard
- **Purpose:** Centralized operational overview.
- **Functionality:** Provides real-time KPIs, charts, and metrics related to orders, revenue, active fleets, and system health.

### 2. POS & Billing
- **Purpose:** Point of Sale system for restaurants and retail.
- **Functionality:** Includes Billing Terminal (order entry), Open/Hold/Split/Merge Bill management, Payment processing, and daily Shift Management.

### 3. Restaurant Management
- **Purpose:** Manages the restaurant business structure.
- **Functionality:** Overview of all restaurant branches, owner registration, and pending approval workflows for new outlets.

### 4. Menu Management
- **Purpose:** Control food and product offerings.
- **Functionality:** Manages categories, individual items, pricing, modifiers (add-ons), combo meals, and approval workflows for menu updates.

### 5. Kitchen Management
- **Purpose:** Backend kitchen operational support.
- **Functionality:** Kitchen Display System (KDS) for order status tracking, live kitchen throughput monitoring, and chef task assignments.

### 6. Order Management
- **Purpose:** Lifecycle management of customer orders.
- **Functionality:** Tracks All Orders (across types: Delivery, Dine-in, Takeaway, Scheduled) and handles Refund/Return processing.

### 7. Table & Reservation
- **Purpose:** Front-of-house table coordination.
- **Functionality:** Manages table status (reserved/occupied), reservation bookings, and QR-based self-ordering configurations.

### 8. Identity & Access (IAM)
- **Purpose:** User and security administration.
- **Functionality:** Manages user roles, permissions, and identity profiles for different system users (Staff, Customers, Riders).

### 9. Customer Management
- **Purpose:** Customer relationship and history.
- **Functionality:** Tracks customer profiles, wallet balances, ride/order history, support tickets, and feedback/ratings.

### 10. Driver Management
- **Purpose:** Comprehensive driver lifecycle.
- **Functionality:** Registration, KYC verification, document management, performance tracking, wallet, and earnings calculations.

### 11. Vehicle Management
- **Purpose:** Managing individual vehicle units.
- **Functionality:** Registration, documentation, insurance tracking, maintenance schedules, and fuel/EV energy management.

### 12. Fleet Management
- **Purpose:** Operational fleet oversight.
- **Functionality:** Manages fleet owners, allocation of vehicles to services, performance analytics, and large-scale maintenance.

### 13. Bike Rental (Lease)
- **Purpose:** Specialized bike rental and leasing operations.
- **Functionality:** Manages inventory, registration, QR allocation, billing, maintenance, and fine/penalty tracking for rentals.

### 14. Ride Operations
- **Purpose:** Core ride-hailing functionality.
- **Functionality:** Booking matching, dispatching, real-time trip tracking, cancellation management, and trip history.

### 15. GPS & Tracking
- **Purpose:** Real-time location intelligence.
- **Functionality:** Live vehicle tracking, ETA calculation, route optimization, and geofencing alerts.

### 16. Pricing & Fare
- **Purpose:** Revenue control and fare logic.
- **Functionality:** Fare rules (per km/time), dynamic surge pricing, coupon/promo management, and toll calculation.

### 17. Payment Management
- **Purpose:** Consolidated payment handling.
- **Functionality:** Processes Cash, UPI, Online payments, handles wallets, refunds, driver payouts, and commission settlements.

### 18. Finance & Accounting
- **Purpose:** Financial bookkeeping and reporting.
- **Functionality:** Revenue tracking, expense management, invoice generation, tax computation, and P&L reports.

### 19. Payroll
- **Purpose:** Employee and driver compensation.
- **Functionality:** Automates driver earnings, salaries, bonuses, incentives, and deductions.

### 20. HR Management
- **Purpose:** Employee lifecycle and workplace management.
- **Functionality:** Employees, attendance, leave, recruitment, training, and performance reviews.

### 21. Procurement & Inventory
- **Purpose:** Managing inputs and supplies.
- **Functionality:** Supplier management, purchase requests, purchase orders, vendor payments, spare parts, and fuel inventory.

### 22. Asset Management
- **Purpose:** Non-fleet physical asset tracking.
- **Functionality:** Manages office assets, computers, mobile devices, and other infrastructure.

### 23. CRM & Help Desk
- **Purpose:** Customer service and support automation.
- **Functionality:** Support ticketing system, complaints, live chat, call logs, and customer feedback management.

### 24. Marketing
- **Purpose:** Growth and engagement.
- **Functionality:** Campaigns, referral programs, coupons, loyalty management, and promotional activities.

### 25. Notifications
- **Purpose:** Automated communication.
- **Functionality:** SMS, Email, Push notifications, WhatsApp integration, and In-App messaging.

### 26. Reports & Analytics
- **Purpose:** Strategic business reporting.
- **Functionality:** Consolidated reports across Revenue, Rides, Drivers, Fleet, and Customers.

### 27. AI & Business Intelligence
- **Purpose:** Advanced algorithmic decision support.
- **Functionality:** AI-based ride matching, demand prediction, fraud detection, heat maps, and ML-driven insights.

### 28. Integrations
- **Purpose:** Third-party platform connectivity.
- **Functionality:** Payment gateways, Maps API, IoT device management, and analytics connectors.

### 29. Subscription & Billing
- **Purpose:** SaaS billing model.
- **Functionality:** Plan management, automated subscription billing, invoices, and renewal tracking.

### 30. Security
- **Purpose:** Platform protection.
- **Functionality:** Audit logs, 2FA, access control, and authentication security.

### 31. Legal & Compliance
- **Purpose:** Regulatory adherence.
- **Functionality:** KYC, driver/vehicle verification, insurance records, contracts, and incident management.

### 32. Settings
- **Purpose:** System-wide configuration.
- **Functionality:** General app config, branding, localization (currency/time), API keys, cache, and advanced AI settings.
